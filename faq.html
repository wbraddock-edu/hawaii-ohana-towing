Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ohana Towing & Recovery.

How fast can you get to me?+
Our average response time on Oahu is under 30 minutes. During peak hours or extreme weather, times may be slightly longer. We'll give you an estimated arrival time when you call and keep you updated throughout.
Do you operate 24/7, including holidays?+
Yes, absolutely. Ohana Towing operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year — including all holidays. Breakdowns don't respect schedules, and neither do we.
How much does towing cost in Honolulu?+
Our base flatbed tow starts at $95 plus $3 per mile. Roadside assistance (jump starts, tire changes, fuel delivery) starts at $65. Lockout service starts at $75. We always quote your price upfront before we begin — no surprise charges.
Do you accept insurance for towing?+
Yes! We work directly with all major insurance companies. If your policy includes roadside assistance, we can usually bill them directly. Have your insurance card ready and we'll handle the paperwork for you.
Can you tow a motorcycle or scooter?+
Yes. We have specialized equipment and trained operators for motorcycle towing. All bikes are secured with professional-grade tie-down straps and transported safely on our flatbed trucks.
Do you cover the whole island of Oahu?+
Yes, we serve all of Oahu — from Haleiwa to Hawaii Kai, Pearl City to Kailua. No matter where you are on the island, we can reach you. Remote locations (Kaena Point area, etc.) may take slightly longer.
What happens to my car after it's towed?+
We take your vehicle to whatever destination you specify — home, a repair shop, a dealership, or our secure holding lot. We communicate clearly throughout and don't release your vehicle without your authorization.
Are your drivers licensed and insured?+
Every driver is fully licensed, insured, and has passed a background check. We are a Hawaii DMV-licensed towing company with full liability insurance on all vehicles we transport.
Can you help with long-distance towing between islands?+
We specialize in Oahu towing, but we can coordinate inter-island vehicle transport through our network of trusted partners. Call us to discuss your situation and we'll find the best solution.
