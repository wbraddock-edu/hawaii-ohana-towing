About Ohana Towing

Family-owned, Honolulu-proud. Serving Oahu since 2004.

Est. 2004

Built on Family Values

Marcus and Alana Kahala started Ohana Towing in 2004 with one truck and a simple promise: respond fast, treat customers like family, and never leave anyone stranded. Two decades later, we've grown to a fleet of 12 trucks and a team of 18 — but that promise hasn't changed.

We've towed vehicles from every corner of Oahu — from the North Shore to Hawaii Kai, from Pearl Harbor to downtown Honolulu. We know these roads, these communities, and the unique challenges of island driving. That local knowledge makes us faster and smarter than any mainland-franchised alternative.

When you call Ohana Towing, you're not a ticket number. You're family.
